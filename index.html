Sokotech Space

The ​factory, which unfolds its wings on two streets in front of a green square, is adjacent to an amazing urban network of small wooded streets, which still retains plant and early-stage houses from the 20th century. In spite of this, SokoTech is located in one of the best connected points of the city, thanks to the neighborhood of subway and suburbs. The space consists of two open spaces: one of 300m2 and 180m2 of industrial style, which houses an office and a Manufacturing Laboratory.
